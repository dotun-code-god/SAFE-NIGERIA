# SAFE-NIGERIA

# **SAFE-NIGERIA | Implementation Strategy**

SAFE-NIGERIA is a national-scale flood intelligence platform designed to provide real-time monitoring, prediction, and early warning capabilities for flood-prone communities across Nigeria.

Unlike traditional flood monitoring solutions that rely primarily on satellite imagery and delayed reporting, SAFE-NIGERIA combines distributed solar-powered monitoring nodes, long-range communication networks, predictive analytics, artificial intelligence, and multi-level government integration to create a proactive disaster prevention ecosystem.

The platform is designed to serve four distinct stakeholder groups:

1. Citizens and Communities  
2. Emergency Response Agencies  
3. Government Institutions  
4. Infrastructure and Enterprise Operators

The long-term vision is to establish Nigeria's most comprehensive flood intelligence network capable of supporting local, state, and federal disaster management operations while making critical flood information accessible to the public.

### **Hardware AI Intelligence Layer**

Edge AI using Gemma

Runs directly on monitoring devices.

Capabilities:

• Sensor anomaly detection  
• False alert reduction  
• Local risk assessment  
• Offline intelligence

Purpose:

Reduce bandwidth requirements and improve resilience.
